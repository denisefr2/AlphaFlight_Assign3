Audio Engineer

Job Brief:

We need an audio engineer to design and edit the sounds for our game. Sound is required for our in-game world for adding more immersive experience for the users. The audio engineer needs to be able to understand and encapsulate the essence of the audio for the game that they are producing.( Dawn Rosenberg Mckay September 06 2019, What Does an Audio Engineer Do?)

Responsibilities:

Collaborating with developers and designers.

Help our team craft a world-class audio experience for our game.

Creating ambient background music, NPC dialogues and other sound effects that bring an immersive experience to our users.

Testing and quality control of in-game audio assets.

Requirements:

At least 2 years previous work experience in game audio engineering.

Excellent communication skills.

Problem Solving skills.

Critical thinking.

In-depth knowledge of waveform editing, equalization, compression, mixing, plug-ins, etc.

Proficiency with audio software such as DAW, Reaper, Nuendo, Sound Forge, Cubase, Pro Tools, Logic, Vegas, etc.

Experience and knowledge of audio game development tools such as Fmod and Wwise.

Knowledge or experience with any visual scripting programs such as Reaktor, Pure Data, UDK, Max, etc.
