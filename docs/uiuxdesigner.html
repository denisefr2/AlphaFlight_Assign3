UI/UX Designer

Job brief:

Our game becomes more entertaining when users enjoy their interactions with the game. We need a UI/UX developer to create intuitive interfaces and well-designed responsive interactions for our game. Thus, providing a comfortable experience for our users while they play the game, allowing them to be more engaged in the gameplay. (Workable n.d, UI/UX Designer job description)

Responsibility:

Ensure that our game has an appealing user interface.

Craft and conceptualize visual aspects that facilitate user’s gameplay.

Drafting graphic designs, choose fonts and determine colour schemes.

Design user flows and wireframes.

Conduct user research and create user personas.

Program and code the designs.

Test our game on real users and gather feedback.

Requirements:

A UI/UX developer should have writing skills, collaboration, and coding skills.

Having a deep knowledge of graphic design.

Knowledge of wireframe tools such as Wireframe.cc and InVision.

Proficient in design programs like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Affinity Designer and CorelDRAW.

Have relevant work experience in the UI/UX designer or similar role.

Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology or relevant field.

Knowledge of programming languages such as C++, JavaScript and HTML.

Previous experience in developing applications and software.
