As an ex-Defence member, I have travelled and lived in most parts of the country and settled down in Western Australia. After a year in this position, I have seen enormous growth in my skills and knowledge as I become more familiar with enterprise IT solutions. I look forward to continuing my IT journey with my team, Alpha Flight.

I’m a gamer. I have been since Wolfenstein 3D in 1992. Gaming is both my hobby and my relaxation. I’m keenly interested in how games are created, both from technological and story telling perspective. My first games were first person shooters but once I discovered role playing games (RPGs), I looked for more story in all types of games. Nothing satisfies me more than being immersed in a digital story that gives that ‘wow that’s clever’ feeling. I’ve been interested in technology and how things work for most of my life. That interest was confined to personal hobbies until the past 20 years or so. In that time I’ve been employed in various IT related roles, including first and second level support, technical training and as a technical specialist. Working with business related front-end technology has only increased my interest and ‘need to know’.
