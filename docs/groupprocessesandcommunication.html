Group Processes and Communitcation

How will your group communicate?

Our group will be using Microsoft Teams and RMIT email/Canvas for all messaging and communications.

How often will meetings take place?

Our meeting takes place twice a week, Wednesday 9 pm AEST and Sunday 8 pm AEST via Microsoft Teams.

Will these be face-to-face, or using technologies such as Skype? Or Facebook? Or email? Or text?

These meetings will all be video conferences, scheduled by Microsoft Teams and a reminder sent out to our RMIT email addresses.

What will you do if you have a group member who does not respond to communications?

If a group member does not respond to our communications within two days, we will bring it to the attention of our tutor.
