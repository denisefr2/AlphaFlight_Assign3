Designs and Development Issues

Poor quality code

Poorly documented.

Do not have the features that it claims it has.

Create coding standards and use best practices for coding.

Test code frequently.

Fix any bug and logical errors that arise.

Steep Learning Curve

Difficult learning curve for some of the software used, such as Houdini and Unreal Engine.

Study a graphic design course.

If we have enough funding we can hire someone with the relevant skills.

Unable to obtain Fundings

Funding is necessary for both the development and continued maintenance; our funding requests may get rejected or not meet our target amount for various reasons.

Draw up a good pitch for our project.

Have contingency plans for different sources of funding.

Work voluntarily or find voluntary employees.

Inaccurate Estimations

We might underestimate the length of our project, and be unable to achieve milestone dates on time, also requiring us to allocate more resources and funding than expected.

Set more realistic goals and taking in the cone of uncertainty into consideration when estimating.

Inability to complete Cat-A-Wall

The development of a game as big as Cat-A-Wall require and number of specialists and time that Alpha Flight does not realistically have in the timeframe given.

Complete as much as we realistically can.

Training

For the Alpha Flight team to conduct the build of a game considerable training would be required. However, in the time allocated this is not feasible.

Work as a team to pivot the skills already dispersed within the team to lessen the burden of training required.

Size of Team

The project was allocated 4-6 members. For no fault of our own we lost members and now have three creating an imbalance in the substantial amount of work and time required to complete this project.

Acknowledge that our teamâ€™s individual time is finite and work to the best they can.

Fact vs Fiction

Inability to ascertain from the assignment what is fact and what is fiction or what is intended vs what is achievable. For example, making a time frame of 15 weeks to complete a project knowing it will not be achieved.

Use sound judgement to show what could be achieved and what we need to show for the purposes of this assignment.

Implementing BYOD

There may be too much financial strain for employees to purchase the relevant hardware required.

Our team will consider purchasing the hardware or choose other software that has fewer specification requirements.
