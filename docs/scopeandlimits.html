Scope and Limits

Our project objective for the allowed timeframe is to complete the planning phase from our development cycle.

We will be able to show planning for:

- Budget

- Funding

- Audience

- Labour

- Timeframes

It is also in our objective to deliver several artifacts including prototype map designs, level design and merchandises.

The estimated time to complete our full project would be 24 months, therefore, our production of work would be limited from the given timeframe of this assignment.

For that reason, we will not be able to produce a fully playable level with the desired details. We cannot deliver or design most of our merchandise. There will only be sample dialogues of our quests.
