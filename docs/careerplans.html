Common Elements

The front-end developer and service delivery manager both require technical skills and knowledge of cloud services/applications. These skills differentiate between the roles by being technically focused as for a developer or the strategic management of a team of developers as for an SDM. Both roles require customer skills such as, interpreting the client’s needs, conveying an achievable plan and successfully delivery a product.

As an SDM there is a requirement to in some situations integrate with a cyber defence operations lead. As both roles are based around providing adequate solutions to the customer they can work closely together to ensure this. The cyber lead will provide advice and suggestions for implementing best practices into a system. The SDM can in turn tasks members of their team to implement suggestions made by the cyber lead.

One element is prevalent across all three jobs as a critical requirement and that is the ability to work in team. Furthermore, all positions highlight a requirement for someone who is able to integrate soundly into a team environment either as a member or supervisor/manager.
