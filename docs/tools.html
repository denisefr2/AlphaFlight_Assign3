Team comments on Tools

GitHub

Teams

Denise

Alpha Flight used GitHub to upload the website pages for assignment 2.

It is a very useful tool for managing multiple users editing the same files.

It can be a little difficult initially, with a rather sharp learning curve, but once mastered, it seems simple enough.

Overall, I feel it’s a tool very suited to purpose.

Alpha Flight used Teams chat to communicate draft changes initially, then website changes as the work expanded.

Documents, images and other relevant material was uploaded in versions to purpose created folders.

It was particularly useful as a chat client, both video and text. We were able to clearly keep track of who was working on what part of the assignment. We were able to leave short notes alerting the others to the work on foot.

Overall, I found Teams a very useful tool for keeping in contact with my fellow group members and keeping track of the work in progress.

As a social tool it was also excellent, I feel it allowed the three of us feel more like a cohesive group despite our geographic distances.

Jennafer

Our team contributed to the building of our website on equal amounts, whether that was by providing the content to go into the website, designing, writing the code or by providing input/feedback.

The GitHub page reflects our team's ability to work together in achieving a common goal.

Microsoft Teams was the backbone for this assignment. Once we had a system in place for version control etc. outputs from the team flowed effortlessly.

The chat function was also crucial in allowing our team to communicate rapidly.

Each member constantly checked in on the chat to get updates on the assignment's progress.

Zhaohe

Looking at GitHub’s audit trail, it reflected how co-ordinated our group is, we all did our separate tasks and were able to merge them seamlessly.

Everyone contributed an equal amount of effort and lent a helping hand when needed.

We used Microsoft team as our communication tool, and we have benefited greatly from the convenience of this software.

Our group communicated effectively and committed our works efficiently. We used the Teams file function to upload our documents and it was essential for producing the text for our website.

Judging by the upload history of Teams file, everyone knew what they were doing and at what stage our assignment was on.
