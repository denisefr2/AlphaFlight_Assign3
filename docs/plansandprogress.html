What is ‘Cat-a-wall’?

Free to play browser based RPG

Developed by people with a passion for gaming and story telling

Why?

Frustration with lack of quality in free to play games

Wish to provide users with a free to play experience that didn’t include monetisation

To see if we could create a game with a strong story and replayability
